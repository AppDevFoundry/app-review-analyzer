'Most Helpful' iOS App reviews can be found here in JSON format:
https://itunes.apple.com/rss/customerreviews/id=<app-id>/sortby=mosthelpful/json

'Most Recent' iOS App reviews can be found here in JSON format:
https://itunes.apple.com/rss/customerreviews/id=<app-id>/sortby=mostrecent/json

There are attributes at the end for "first", "last", "previous", "next", as the results are paginated.

To retrieve page 2 for example of the 'Most Helpful', you would call:
https://itunes.apple.com/us/rss/customerreviews/page=2/id=<app-id>/sortby=mosthelpful/json
